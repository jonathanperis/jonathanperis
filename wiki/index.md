# Jonathan Peris -- Developer Wiki

Welcome to the comprehensive wiki documenting Jonathan Peris's open-source work, technical decisions, and engineering philosophy.

## Who Is Jonathan Peris?

Software engineer based in **Itanhaem, SP, Brazil**, working at **Peris Studio**. A polyglot developer whose primary expertise lies in **.NET / C# backend systems** but who routinely crosses language boundaries into Rust, Go, C, Python, and TypeScript -- not out of trend-chasing, but to genuinely understand what each ecosystem does best.

Jonathan's repos share a common DNA: every project is **benchmarked**, **containerized**, and **documented**. Whether it's a microservices reference architecture or a 2D platformer, the same engineering rigor applies.
